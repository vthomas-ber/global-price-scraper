Field & Flag Reference

Price Type Flags

Regular

Regular / base price — standard consumer shelf price including VAT. Not a promotion.

Promo-only

Promotional price only — no regular price was visible at the time of scraping. The RSV field will be empty.

[Niche]

Niche / specialist retailer — not a mainstream grocery chain (e.g. health stores, pet shops, independent online retailers).

[D2C]

Direct-to-Consumer — price sourced from the brand's own website or webshop.

[Clearance]

Clearance retailer — discount / end-of-line outlet. Price may not reflect standard retail.

[Name Match Only]

Matched by product name — EAN was not visible on the page. Matched using brand + product title + pack size.

Non-comparable

Cannot calculate per-unit price — pack count not explicitly stated on the source page.

Column Definitions

RSV (Retail Selling Value) — the regular consumer-facing price including VAT. This is the price a shopper pays on the shelf.

Calculated Per-Unit RSV — the cost per 100 g or 100 ml, calculated as: (RSV ÷ pack size in g/ml) × 100. Allows direct price comparison across different pack sizes. Shown as "Non-comparable" when pack count is not explicitly stated.

Average RSV — Standard Retail — mean RSV across Regular and [Niche]/[Name Match Only] sources, excluding D2C and Clearance.

Average RSV — Total Market — mean RSV across all valid sources including D2C and Clearance.

Note on averages: Promo-only and Non-comparable rows are excluded from all average calculations. Averages are shown inline at the end of each EAN's rows in the table below.
